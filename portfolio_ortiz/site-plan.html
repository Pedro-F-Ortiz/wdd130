Overview

Purpose

The purpose for this site plan is to prepare a web page that shows my professional portfolio so that I can show my employer and future job interviews. I have learned that in the tech world, what says a lot is what you have been doing with your time outside of school. So, my plan is to have an online portfolio where I will be able to upload my most current resume and store previous ones, my web site projects and present projects I am working on. In a past interview, I was asked to what projects I had and all I could do is tell but this will allow me to show them what I do with my spare time.

Audience

My audience is going to be my future employer and future companies that I will be interviewing with while exploring my career options. The intent is to have an actual project that I can present with my computer during interviews when asked about what projects have, I done. This will allow me to have a more tech driven job interview. It will also help me keep track of my web site projects and code I am working on all saved in one place.

Content

Home page

My site will consist of an “About me” section that will be focused on my life and how I came to be a software engineer. I will add details about my family and what I like to do in my personal time like fishing, camping, shooting guns and site seeing. This will help paint a mental picture of who I am outside of work during my interview with the hopes of sparking up conversation that will be an ice breaker during the interview. Information that my portfolio will need is a header, body, and footer. Within the header you will see a logo, quote and buttons that will jump you to certain sections of my page like a resume button that will bring up my current resume in pdf form. The about me, projects, and contact me section. The body will contain the About me, and projects section and the footer will contain my contact information like social medial links, trademark, and contact information. I really want my audience to have an image of me before the interview so that I can catch their attention prior to showing up. Considering that this is my first portfolio, it will be very basic, but my hopes is that by the time I graduate I have revised it a lot more and have added more detail to it.
