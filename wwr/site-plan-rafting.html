Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help those interested in finding a new hobby, weekend trip or exciting time by providing a white water rafting experience like no other. We welcome customers of all ages and with all levels of experience. This website will help get the word out to the public of the many resources available in the area to go white water rafting. We need to provide cost per trip, equipment and locations. We need to provide detailed pages with images, cost and testimonies. We want our audience to visit our site via mobile devices, laptops, and desktops.

Audience

We want to get the attention of adventurous people who like the outdoors and don't mind some fun in the water. The age will most likely be teens to middle aged people and couples looking for new things to try during their vacation trips to this side of the states. As the site is built, it is important to keep in mind the outdoor enthusiast and adventurous type of people who are always looking for a new adventure. Considering the nature of the site, I would expect spring colors that are inviting yet some colors that draw attention to our purpose of adventure.
